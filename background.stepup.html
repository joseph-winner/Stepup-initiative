BackGround

Step Up Initiative is a Community Base Civil Society Organization, non-profit making body and non-political organization founded in November 2010 in Aweil with the aim of providing services and resources that enhance the capacity of impoverished communities at both grassroots and urban areas.

STEP UP is focusing on community development at the grassroots particularly Agriculture/food security & livelihood, community security & community policing, peace-building, social protections (child protections, Gender Base Violence), water & sanitation, peace-building, Children issues, Gender issues, awareness raising, human right, civic education, emergency response and organizational development.

It is registered with both National Relief & Rehabilitation Commission (RRC) and States Relief and Rehabilitation Commission (RRC) in the State of operations and has head of office in Kampala.

Goals

To boost community involvement & partnership for peace and development through improved services delivery (Education, Health Food security/livelihoods and infrastructural developments), community security, community policing, peace building, civic engagement, human rights, women empowerment, child protection and disaster/emergency management activities.
