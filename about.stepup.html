ABOUT STEP UP INITIATIVE

WE GIVE YOUNG GIRLS WOMEN AND MOTHERS A FOUNDATION FOR A SUCESSFULL LIFE

Who We Are

Step Up Youth Initiative is a non-profit organization that brings care to Young girls and women and mothers around Uganda the world.

We empower women, youth, and families to effectively navigate through change, raise healthy children and develop strong relationships. We offer regular, consistent support to those in need so that the women in our care can develop over time, giving them the chance to create a brighter future.

What We Do

We provide support to women through; education and family strengthening services.
